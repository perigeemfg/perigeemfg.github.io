About Perigee Manufacturing

A Brief History of Perigee

In 1965, Perigee Manufacturing Company, Inc. was founded in Detroit by Patrick J. Topolewski. We began with 1 Acme-Gridley screw machine and 1 Warner-Swasey turret lathe in a 3,000 square foot building. In 1977, Patrick brought in his son, Michael, to help out as business continued to grow. In 1985, Michael became president and expanded to accommodate 35,000 sq. ft. of building to house 19 screw machines, 18 nut tappers, 12 nut slotters, and a myriad of presses, drills, grinders, and lathes. Michael brought in his son, Michael in 1999 and his son Timothy in 2004. In recent years, Perigee Manufacturing has added a CNC and packaging machine to increase our breadth in the fastener market.

 

Perigee Manufacturing Co., Inc., a family owned and operated company established in 1965, specializes in Hex Nuts Milled from Bar. Our unique manufacturing and process capabilities allow us to satisfy the most demanding product requirements.

Slotting

We are slotting specialists! We can slot, re-tap and finish process your supplied product to print or specification. From slotted Hex nuts and Castle nuts to All-metal (Marsden) Locking Nuts and non-standards (to print), we are capable of tailoring your products to ensure a superior quality fastener with a best-in-class lead time.

Quality

We are dedicated to providing our customers with product of the highest quality, on time, every time. By utilizing stringent process controls, coupled with our on-site test and inspection lab, we are able to achieve full compliance with all state-of-the-art Quality Systems and Government safety and regulatory requirements. Statistical methods and analysis, advanced quality planning, and continuous improvements coupled with our knowledge base are just some of the tools employed in attaining these goals.

Custom-Made Fasteners

While cold forming is capable of producing large quantities of fasteners, it is not cost effective in lower volumes. From 1 piece to 250,000+ pieces, whether plain finish or heat-treated and plated, Perigee Manufacturing will satisfy all your demands. With our new packaging line, we can label, barcode, and bag the finished product to meet your requirements. We truly are a one-stop shop!

Capabilities

What we can do for your company or application

Perigee Manufacturing Company, Inc., is a domestic manufacturer of both screw-machined and CNC-machined products, predominantly hexagon, square and round nuts.

Perigee manufactures small, medium and large volumes in both standard and specialty patterns. Our specialty is slotted and castellated hexagon nuts; additionally we manufacture finished full, jam, heavy, thick, wheel, barrel, prevailing torque/all-metal lock, marsden lock, dynamic lock, cap/acorn, airframe, check, test, tamper-proof, breakaway, panel, sleeve, spacer and various other styles of nuts. We also provide slot milling for customer supplied hexagon nuts in large volumes for the automotive sector. We have more recently added prototype machining capabilities.

Perigee supplies the following sectors/markets: Commercial, Automotive, Automotive Aftermarket, Heavy Truck, Military/Aerospace/Defense, Agricultural, Industrial and Construction. Our QMS is ISO 9001:2008; we have also been certified to the Defense Logistics Agency's Qualified Suppliers List as an approved defense manufacturer since 2002 (CAGE 1X4X7).
